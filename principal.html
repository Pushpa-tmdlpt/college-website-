Principal's Desk

Hard work and discipline are the unerring roads towards success. Therefore our principle of student development is based on three D's - Determination, Dedication, and Discipline, so that they can excel in their respective fields. Our responsibility is not only to create abundant opportunities by offering the very best educational programs but also to provide comprehensive and accessible student services to foster their success, thereby helping them to get good jobs and even facilitate them to become innovative startups and entrepreneurs. That's why we strive to endeavor beyond the boundaries of mere books. We ensure that the students of Gokula Krishna College of Engineering will prove themselves to be not only well-qualified professionals but also responsible and ideal citizens with humanity.
